Titanic Kaggle Project. Data Science.

The "Hello World" of Data Analysis, is the Titanic Kaggle Challenge, and also my first attempt at working with and attempting to predict the survival rate of Titanic passengers.

In this Challenge, you get tasked to complete the analysis of what sorts of people were likely to survive, even though the statistics are available on the web with a quick google search, the main goal is to apply the tools of machine learning to predict which passengers survived the tragedy and compare your outcome with the real figures.

My accuracy rate was about 74%, a good start, but will definitely have to work on improving my ranking.
