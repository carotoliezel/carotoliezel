About Me

Hi there, I'm Liezel, a self taught developer from Johannesburg, South Africa and in the process of obtaining my MSCA: Database Developer certification. Currently working as a QA Engineer and fell in love with creating automated test scripts.

My current focus is on learning the ins and outs of becoming a data analyst. I also have degrees in Criminal Psychology and Law so maybe one day I'd be able to provide insights in preventing crime, it would make my mother proud that I'm finally putting my degrees to good use. But for now I'm just focussed on creating a decent portfolio.
